Why Star Wars Is The Best Franchise

Star Wars is a massive company, that is now owned by Disney. Star Wars was a hit from the start, with the communities love for it never really getting low. Star Wars has done so much with its community that it's impact may never fade. Star Wars was originally made by George Lucas, with the first movie, Star Wars: A new Hope, originally just titled Star Wars was released. Disney obtained Star Wars in 2012, when George Lucas sold Lucasfilm to Disney for $4.05 billion dollars. Star Wars has an estimated $46.7 billion in revenue, including merchandise, movies, toys, etc.

Star Wars has become such an iconic franchise, it has made it's way into other forms of media and entertainment. It's had multiple collaborations with popular video games and toys. Some of these include

  • Fortnite
  • Fall Guys
  • Rocket League
  • Minecraft
  • LEGO
  • And many more
